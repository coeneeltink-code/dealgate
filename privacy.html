DealGate ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard data when you use our platform at dealgate.app.

1. Information We Collect

From freelancers (account holders)

  • Name, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Payment and billing information (processed by Stripe — we never store card data)
  • Intake page configuration: your screening questions, pricing, and profile info
  • Usage data: login timestamps, dashboard interactions

From leads (clients submitting intake forms)

  • Name, email address, and any answers provided in the intake form
  • Payment confirmation data from Stripe (amount, timestamp, last 4 digits)
  • IP address and browser information for fraud prevention

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Operate and maintain the DealGate platform
  • Deliver intake submissions to the relevant freelancer's dashboard
  • Process payments and issue refunds via Stripe
  • Send transactional emails (submission confirmations, receipts)
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Improve our product based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in the European Union using Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security enabled. Data in transit is encrypted via TLS 1.3. Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe and subject to their PCI DSS compliance.

4. Data Sharing

We share data with the following trusted processors:

  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
  • Supabase — database hosting (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Vercel — platform hosting

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@dealgate.app. We respond within 30 days.

6. Data Retention

We retain account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is removed within 30 days. Intake submissions are retained for 12 months or until deleted by the freelancer. Stripe retains transaction records per their own legal obligations.

7. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for authentication (session token). We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify account holders by email at least 14 days before the change takes effect.
